Do the right thing

  1. It is important to do the right thing so I have a good image and that is just who I am.
  2. This came from my mom as she has always done the right thing in my life and I strive to be just like her.
  3. Doing the right thing is important because I want to be treated like I treat other people.
  4. If you do not do the right thing your image will slowly go down as people don't exactly rely on you. You won't get a promotion and you won't have the right people around you.

Respect

  1. Respect is the foundation of companies and life, you have people you respect for how they got there and what they've done.
  2. I gained this from my mother she always taught me to listen to people that know what they're doing and I gained respect for them which made me want people to respect me but I have to earn that first.
  3. Respect is important and I believe it is what holds relationships and companies together is respect for each other.
  4. If society does not have respect then hierarchies will not be a thing and many businesses will collapse and so will relationships.

Adapting

  1. I believe adapting can be benificial to all so you can keep your composure in any situation
  2. This was gained through doing continuous actions and life and never going the way I wanted to so I had to adapt.
  3. It is important to adapt because if you don't you will struggle in life and never be able to overcome any challenge that comes your way
  4. If you can adapt in real life you will not succeed in any part of life especially work and you will not be rellied apon

Standards

  1. Standards is how the world rotates if there is not standards there is no regulation and knowing what to expect.
  2. This value is from my dad, he has instillied in me that there is a standard for everything and if it is not followed you will not be respected.
  3. Standards are important for society because this are cleaned to a standard things are done to standard so we have balance in life.
  4. Standards are the foundation for how life is. If you do not follow standards by a minimum you are not respected. That will hurt how people think of you and your image.
