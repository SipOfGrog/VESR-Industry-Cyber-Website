Data leaks and Ddos attacks

  • Data leaks have happened at playstation before causing problems like customer data that has stored that with playstation
  • There was a Data breach in 2011 that costed Playstation $77 million. That is a major cost to the company and my position.
  • Ddos attacks have also effected Playstation in the past. It has caused major disruptions in the customers who have tried playing during those times.
  • While Ddos attacks don't target data, it can still effect the company if it last long enough. There have been instances in the past where it has lasted long enough to have financial impacts

Best cybersecurity practices

  • Make users and Workers use MFA to access any sort of data.
  • Regularly update systems and consoles to prevent breaches
  • Use firewalls to protect the console and company from any attacks.
  • End to end encryption for all data so no data is leaked from either side of the transaction. Especially for payment and personal information.
